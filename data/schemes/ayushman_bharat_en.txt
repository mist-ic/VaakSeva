# Ayushman Bharat - PM Jan Arogya Yojana

## Overview
AB-PMJAY is the world's largest health insurance scheme fully financed by the government. Launched on September 23, 2018, it provides health coverage to over 55 crore beneficiaries.

## Benefits
- Health cover of Rs 5 lakh per family per year
- Cashless and paperless treatment at empanelled hospitals
- Coverage for 1,949+ medical procedures
- Pre-hospitalization (3 days) and post-hospitalization (15 days) expenses covered

## Eligibility
- Families identified in SECC 2011 database
- Rural: no proper house, manual scavengers, marginalized groups, SC/ST households
- Urban: rag pickers, beggars, domestic workers, street vendors, construction workers

## Coverage Details
- All pre-existing conditions covered from day 1
- Hospitalization, surgery, chemotherapy, dialysis
- Mental health treatment included
- ICU charges covered
- Day-care treatments (no hospitalization required)

## How to Get Ayushman Card
1. Visit pmjay.gov.in or call 14555
2. Click "Am I Eligible"
3. Enter ration card/mobile number
4. If eligible, visit nearest CSC for biometric verification
5. Ayushman card issued

## Find Empanelled Hospital
- pmjay.gov.in/hospitals -> Find Hospital
- Enter pincode to see nearby hospitals

## Helpline
