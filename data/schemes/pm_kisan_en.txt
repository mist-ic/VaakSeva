# PM-KISAN Scheme

## Overview
The Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN) provides direct income support to all land-holding farmer families in India. The scheme became operational from December 1, 2018.

## Financial Benefit
- Rs 6,000 per year in three equal installments of Rs 2,000 each
- Transferred directly to bank accounts via Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT)
- Payment schedule: April-July, August-November, December-March

## Eligibility
- Farmer families holding cultivable land
- Family includes husband, wife, and minor children (below 18 years)

## Exclusion Categories
- Institutional land holders
- Former/current constitutional post holders (Ministers, MPs, MLAs)
- Retired/serving Central/State government officers (Group A/B)
- Retired pensioners with monthly pension above Rs 10,000
- Income tax payers
- Professionals (doctors, engineers, lawyers, CAs)

## Required Documents
- Aadhaar card (mandatory)
- Bank account linked with Aadhaar
- Land ownership records (Khasra/Khatauni)
- Mobile number

## Application Process
1. Visit pmkisan.gov.in
2. Click "New Farmer Registration"
3. Fill Aadhaar number, mobile, and bank details
4. Submit land holding details
5. Can also apply through nearest CSC (Common Service Centre)
6. Registration via Village Patwari/Lekhpal also possible

## Check Payment Status
- Visit pmkisan.gov.in -> Beneficiary Status
- Enter Aadhaar or bank account number
- PM Kisan Mobile App available
